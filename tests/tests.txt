# Tests

## Test 1 - App loads and shows QR client screen [sanity]
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies the public client entry screen loads.

Steps:
1. Open the app
2. Wait for the page to load
3. Look for the queue registration form

Expected: The "Entrar na fila" form is visible with name, WhatsApp, quantity and observations fields.

## Test 2 - Client can enter the queue
Viewport: mobile
Description: Verifies a client can submit the QR queue form and see their waiting status.

Steps:
1. Open the app on mobile
2. Fill the client queue form
3. Tap "Entrar na fila agora"

Expected: The client status screen appears and says the client is waiting to be called.

## Test 3 - Reception panel shows queue and suggestions
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies staff can access the reception view.

Steps:
1. Open the app
2. Click "Recepcao"
3. Review the waiting list and ready-table section

Expected: The reception panel shows clients in the queue and ready tables with suggested compatible clients.

## Test 4 - Waiter panel shows table map
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies staff can access the waiter table map.

Steps:
1. Open the app
2. Click "Garcom"
3. Review the table cards

Expected: The waiter panel shows table numbers, capacities, sectors and table statuses.

## Test 5 - Admin panel shows reports and fit rules
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies the admin area exposes operational settings.

Steps:
1. Open the app
2. Click "Admin"
3. Review reports and queue compatibility settings

Expected: The admin panel shows report cards, tables, employees and fit-rule controls.
